## 🎯 Why I Built This

Most people preparing for M9A rely on rereading notes. That doesn't work. **Active recall does.**

Instead of passively reviewing material, this app forces you to retrieve information from memory — the single most effective study technique backed by cognitive science. I built this during my own M9A preparation because I wanted a tool that mirrors the real exam experience, not just another PDF.

---

## ✨ Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🎲 **Randomised Questions** | 50 questions per session, drawn randomly from a structured question bank |
| ⏱️ **Timed Sessions** | Simulates real exam pressure with a countdown timer |
| ✅ **Instant Feedback** | See correct/incorrect answers immediately after each response |
| 📊 **Score & Pass/Fail** | Final score calculated against the official 70% pass benchmark |
| 📱 **Mobile-Friendly** | Fully responsive — study on your phone, tablet, or desktop |
| 🔁 **Unlimited Attempts** | Retry as many times as needed — no login, no paywall, no limits |
| 📖 **Exam-Mirrored Format** | Question bank structured to reflect real M9A exam topics |

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Load** — App initialises and shuffles the full question bank
2. **Practice** — 50 randomised MCQ questions served one at a time
3. **Answer** — Select your answer and receive instant feedback
4. **Score** — Final score displayed with pass/fail status against the 70% benchmark
5. **Retry** — Start a new session with a fresh randomised set

---

## 📋 About M9A

The **MAS M9A** (Rules and Regulations for the Conduct of Life Insurance Business) is a mandatory CMFAS module for financial advisors in Singapore who wish to sell life insurance products. Candidates must score **70% or above** to pass.

This app is designed as a **supplementary study tool** — not an official MAS or IBF resource.
